Professional Profile

Holding an MA in TEFL and a BA in Linguistics (Magna Cum Laude, Ohio State University), my instructional approach bridges rigorous computational phonology with practical adult language acquisition.


My pedagogical framework is deeply informed by extensive personal language acquisition. Drawing from fluency in Spanish (DELE C1) and advanced Catalan, alongside comparative structural studies in Japanese, French, Portuguese, and Italian, I leverage structural breakdowns and core linguistic principles to clarify the mechanics of English.


I specialize in systematic scaffolding for foundational and basic-level learners. By actively managing cognitive load and utilizing highly structured input paradigms, I ensure rapid, measurable acquisition for students starting at A1/A2 levels, while scaling these same principles for advanced corporate communications.
