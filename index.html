About Me

Hello! I'm Carson Kane, an ESL Teacher based in Columbus, OH. I am TEFL Certified and passionate about helping adults learn and improve their English skills.

My background is in Linguistics (Bachelors' Degree from The Ohio State University, Magna Cum Laude, specializing in computational phonology), which gives me a deep understanding of language structure and learning.

I spent 8 months living and studying in Spain, achieving fluency in Spanish (DELE certified) and an advanced level in Catalan. I'm also learning Japanese (Intermediate) and Portuguese (Intermediate).
