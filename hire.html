Core Institutional Offerings

High-Energy Conversation Classes

I naturally bring a high energy and conversational focus to the classroom. Through natural conversation, positive error correction, and successful interactions that encourage learning, students gain confidence in their abilities.

Advanced Task-Based Instruction

For B1/B2 learners, I design complex, real-world scenarios using the Task-Based Language Teaching (TBLT) framework. Students must negotiate meaning and collaborate to solve problems, to help them escape the intermediate plateau and enter high stages of learning.

English for Specific Purposes (ESP)

I offer curriculum creation and personalized courses for various sectors (i.e., Environmental Law). This includes teaching that minimizes Teacher Talking Time (TTT), encourages peer interaction as much as possible, and uses authentic material whenever it is relevant.

Domain Expertise: Technology & Environmental Law

I offer specialized language instruction based on your needs. One of my specialties focuses on modern digital workflows, green technology transitions, and environmental legislation.

  • Custom curriculum development for Environmental Law, focusing on complex geopolitical topics like decarbonization, mineral supply chains, and green energy.
  • Integration of local LLMs into language practice and multilingual translation.
  • Instructional design using technical proficiencies in Python, JavaScript, and HTML.
